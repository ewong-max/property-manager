================================================================================
  PROPERTY MANAGER
  A Digital Rental Property Management System
================================================================================

INTRODUCTION
------------
Property Manager is a desktop application designed for Malaysian property
investors and investment holding companies to manage their rental property
portfolios efficiently. It consolidates property records, tenancy agreements,
income tracking, and expense management into a single system — and generates
tax-ready financial statements at the click of a button.

The application runs locally on your computer, keeping all data private and
accessible without an internet connection.


================================================================================
KEY FEATURES
================================================================================

1. MULTI-COMPANY PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------
  - Manage multiple investment holding companies (Sdn Bhd) under one system
  - Each company maintains its own portfolio of properties
  - View consolidated or per-company reporting


2. PROPERTY MANAGEMENT
-----------------------
  - Record full property details: address, type, title type (Freehold/Leasehold)
  - Track purchase price, purchase date, annual assessment, and quit rent values
  - Supports both residential and commercial property types
  - Quick overview of occupancy status across all properties


3. TENANCY MANAGEMENT
----------------------
  - Maintain complete tenancy records including tenant name, IC/SSM number,
    and contact details
  - Track rental amount, deposit, tenancy start and end dates
  - Record tenancy terminations with reason and date
  - Full history of past and present tenancies per property


4. RENTAL INCOME TRACKING
--------------------------
  - Record monthly rental payments against each tenancy
  - Supports both Rental Income and Forfeited Deposit Income entries
  - Monthly breakdown provides a clear picture of cash collection
  - Instantly identify months with missing or outstanding payments


5. EXPENSE MANAGEMENT
----------------------
  - Record property expenses by category:
      • Repair & Maintenance
      • Assessment (Cukai Pintu)
      • Quit Rent (Cukai Tanah)
      • Insurance
      • Management Fee
      • Utilities
      • Professional Fee
      • Other
  - Attach invoice files (PDF, JPG, PNG) to each expense record
  - When Assessment or Quit Rent is selected, the system automatically
    pre-fills the amount from the property record

  AI INVOICE ANALYSIS (Powered by Google Gemini)
  -----------------------------------------------
  Upload a scanned invoice or receipt and the system automatically reads and
  fills in the expense details — vendor name, invoice number, date, amount,
  and category. Supports PDF and image formats. Saves time and reduces
  manual data entry errors.


6. DASHBOARD & ANALYTICS
-------------------------
  - Year of Assessment (YA) selector to view any year's performance
  - Portfolio-wide summary: total income, total expenses, and net rental income
  - Property cards showing each property's YA income, expenses, net figure,
    and current tenant at a glance
  - Historical Performance Chart displaying the last 6 years of gross income,
    total expenses, and net income as a trend chart
  - Quick-action buttons on each property card:
      • Add Income  — opens income entry directly for that property's tenant
      • Add Expense — opens expense entry pre-filled with that property
      • View Report — jumps straight to that property's financial statement
      • Excel Export — downloads the property's Excel statement instantly


================================================================================
REPORTING
================================================================================

The reporting module generates formal rental income statements suitable for
tax filing under the Malaysian Income Tax Act 1967 (ITA 1967).

YEAR OF ASSESSMENT (YA) STATEMENTS
-------------------------------------
Each statement covers one Year of Assessment and is structured in four parts:

  Part A — Property Information
    Details of the property, owner/company, address, and tenancy particulars
    for the assessment year.

  Part B — Gross Rental Income
    Month-by-month rental income table showing amounts received for each
    month of the year, with a full-year total.

  Part C — Allowable Expenses
    Expenses grouped by category, with individual line items and subtotals
    per category. Categories follow standard Malaysian rental income
    deductible expense classifications.

  Part D — Net Rental Income
    Summary showing Gross Income less Total Expenses, arriving at the
    Net Rental Income figure for the Year of Assessment.


MULTI-PROPERTY REPORTING
--------------------------
  - Select any combination of properties for a combined report
  - "Select All" option generates a report covering the entire portfolio
    in a single document
  - Each property's statement appears as a separate section
  - All properties share the same Year of Assessment for easy comparison


EXPORT OPTIONS
--------------
  PDF Export
    Generates a professionally formatted PDF statement ready for submission
    or filing. Multi-property selections produce a single PDF with one
    statement per page.

  Excel Export (Single Property)
    Downloads an Excel workbook (.xlsx) for the selected property and YA,
    containing income and expense details in spreadsheet format for further
    analysis or record-keeping.

  Excel Export (Multiple Properties)
    Downloads a single Excel workbook containing:
      • A Summary sheet comparing all selected properties side by side
      • Individual sheets per property with full income and expense detail


================================================================================
GETTING STARTED
================================================================================

1. Launch the application by double-clicking PropertyManager.exe
2. Open your browser to http://localhost:3001 (opens automatically)
3. Log in with your PIN (default: 1234 — change in config.env)
4. Add your company under the Companies menu
5. Add your properties under Properties
6. Add tenancy agreements and start recording income and expenses
7. Generate reports from the Reports menu or directly from the Dashboard


CONFIGURATION
-------------
Edit the config.env file (located in the same folder as PropertyManager.exe)
to change settings:

  PIN            Your login PIN (default: 1234)
  PORT           Network port (default: 3001)
  GEMINI_API_KEY Your Google Gemini API key (required for AI invoice analysis)
